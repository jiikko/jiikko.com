Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2024

Multi-Video Player for Dropbox ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Dropbox Integration

Through Dropbox OAuth authentication, the App accesses the following information:

  • File listings in your Dropbox account (file names, paths, modification dates, and other metadata)
  • Thumbnail images of video files
  • Temporary download links for video files (for streaming playback)

This information is used solely to provide the App's functionality.

1.2 Locally Stored Information

Data Purpose Storage Location
Dropbox authentication token Maintaining Dropbox access Your device
App settings Preserving layout, volume, etc. Your device
Workspace information Restoring work state Your device
Thumbnail cache Improving display speed Your device (memory)

1.3 Subscription Information (Paid Version)

When purchased through the Mac App Store, the following information is processed through Apple's systems:

  • Purchase receipts (for subscription verification)
  • Subscription expiration dates

This information is used only to verify subscription status. No personally identifiable information is shared with the App developer.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying and playing video files from your Dropbox
  • Saving and restoring user settings and workspaces
  • Verifying subscription status (paid version)
  • Improving app functionality (debugging when errors occur)

3. Information Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

However, minimal information is transmitted through integration with the following services:

  • Dropbox: Authentication credentials for file access
  • Apple: Subscription purchase and verification (paid version only)

Please refer to each service's website for their privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to servers operated by the App developer
  • All communication with Dropbox is encrypted via HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
  • Authentication tokens are stored in the system's secure storage

5. Data Deletion

Users can delete their data through the following methods:

  • Disconnect Dropbox: Use the "Logout" button in the App to delete authentication tokens
  • Complete data deletion: Uninstall the App
  • Revoke access from Dropbox: Remove the App from Dropbox Connected Apps

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated without prior notice due to legal changes or new features. Significant changes will be notified within the App.
